REFUND AND CANCELLATION POLICY:
1. Cancellations made 48 hours before departure are eligible for a 90% refund.
2. Cancellations made within 24 hours of departure will receive a 50% refund.
3. No refunds are processed after the train has departed.

BAGGAGE RULES:
- Sleeper Class passengers are allowed up to 40kg of luggage.
- AC Class passengers are allowed up to 70kg of luggage.
- Pets are allowed only in AC 1 Tier coupes and must be booked in advance.

SENIOR CITIZEN CONCESSION:
- Citizens over 60 years are eligible for a 40% discount on base fare.
- Valid ID proof must be carried during the journey.
